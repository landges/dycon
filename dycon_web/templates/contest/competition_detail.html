We invite you to participate in the Dialogue 2021 competition on Russian News Clustering and Headline Generation. The task consists of three tracks: clustering, headline selection, and headline generation. This competition aims to collect and compare different approaches to text clustering and headline selection.

We took news documents from the Telegram Data Clustering Contest and collected annotations via Yandex.Toloka crowdsourcing platform.

The competition consists of three stages:

  • Clustering (this page)
  • Choosing the best headline (https://competitions.codalab.org/competitions/29479)
  • Generating cluster headline (https://competitions.codalab.org/competitions/29905)

Discussion group in Telegram: https://t.me/dialogue_clustering

Important dates

  • Release of the Clustering data: February 8, 2021
  • Release of the side-by-side titles comparison data: February 26, 2021
  • Deadline for all submissions: March 21, 2021

Description

In the Clustering stage, you have given a collection of news documents from the Telegram Data Clustering Contest and a set of pairs from these documents. Your task is, for each given pair, to predict whether two documents describe the same event. We provide the labeled training dataset and the unlabeled test dataset.

Evaluation Criteria

The main metric F-score for positive examples.

Data

Training set: https://www.dropbox.com/s/8lu6dw8zcrn840j/ru_clustering_0525_urls.tsv

Test set: https://www.dropbox.com/s/v2lbfft6pgzoeq3/ru_clustering_test_fixed.tsv

Columns

  • first_url - URL of the first document
  • second_url - URL of the second document
  • quality - "OK" if two documents describe the same event, "BAD" otherwise
  • dataset - exists only in test dataset to distinguish different dates
